Frequently Asked Questions

How do I upgrade my subscription?

You can upgrade your subscription at any time by logging into your account, navigating to the subscription page, and selecting your desired plan. Your new benefits will be available immediately, and you'll only be charged the prorated difference for the remainder of your billing period.

Can I downgrade my subscription?

Yes, you can downgrade your subscription at any time. Changes will take effect at the end of your current billing cycle. You'll continue to have access to your current plan's features until that time.

Are there any discounts for annual subscriptions?

Yes! We offer a 20% discount when you choose annual billing for either the Gold or Platinum plans. This option will be available during checkout.

What happens to my saved recipes if I cancel my subscription?

Your saved recipes will remain in your account, even if you downgrade or cancel. However, you'll only be able to access recipes that are available to your current subscription level.

How do the interactive cooking sessions work?

Platinum members can schedule virtual cooking sessions with our Ethiopian cuisine experts. These are live, interactive sessions where you can follow along and get real-time feedback and tips. Sessions are available weekly and can be booked through your account dashboard.
